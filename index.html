AB*21 is a Web-Radio from the Brussels Art School La Cambre. We are three students from different artistic fields and countries. We want to implement a place to experiment and express themselves. For students, teachers, alumnus, friends and outside. It can vary from text and spoken word, to podcast, musical practice or any other artistic practice. We see ourselves also as an educational platform and amplifier of student thoughts and needs.
